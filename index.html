MLB Grand Salami Tracker

A daily tracker and predictive model for MLB Grand Salami totals — the combined runs scored across every game on today's slate.

What is the Grand Salami?

Sportsbooks post a single Over/Under for the total runs across all MLB games on a given day. You bet that the slate's total runs will finish over or under that number.

Where to bet it

The Grand Salami is available on BetOnline, Bovada, and DraftKings — though it's posted most consistently on BetOnline. Lines can differ by 1–2 runs book-to-book, so line shopping matters.

Picks

Picks are generated based on model-identified outliers (either 12+ runs or 5 or under). A 🔥 or ❄️ on the game card shows which games the model has identified.

This is a personal project, not financial advice. Always bet responsibly.
